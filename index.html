Our Chapel

Welcome to Christ the King Church, located in the serene village of Christurajapuram, Marthandam, in the Kanyakumari district of Tamil Nadu. For over 35 years, our chapel has stood as a bastion of the traditional Catholic faith in Southern India.

We are exclusively dedicated to the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass in its immemorial Roman Rite (the 1962 Missal), ensuring that the timeless liturgy, traditional sacraments, and unmodified teachings of the Church prior to Vatican II are preserved and passed down to future generations.

Our chapel is run by the priests of the Society of Saint Pius X (SSPX).
